# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a **respectful, inclusive, and open** environment.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project and community harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of different viewpoints
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory jokes or language

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported via GitHub Issues or email (add contact later).  
Maintainers will review and take appropriate action.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
